The Ultimate Luxury Tea Experience

Sadie's Tearooms are located at the heart of Greystones Village in Co.Wicklow. Just a short walk from the seafront, it is the perfect location to unwind for the afternoon. We specialise in providing a luxury Afternoon Tea experience. Indulge in some freshly baked treats in our tearooms alongside a selection of Teas and Coffees. We serve in our Tearooms from 12-5pm, Monday-Sunday.


In recent times, we know with closures it hasn't been easy to always celebrate occasions. As a result, we have introduced an order online feature. You can order for collection to bring your luxury experience anywhere to be shared and enjoyed.


We offer Vegan and Gluten-free options with our Afternoon Tea. Please let us know when ordering. Online orders can be done for up to 6 people. Contact us directly for larger group orders.
